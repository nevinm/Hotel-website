FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

GENERAL QUESTIONS

What is On-Demand Delivery?

Yes, we deliver when you want to cook. No subscription and planning ahead. No storage. No waste.

If you order for “NOW”, we typically deliver within 20-40 minutes. We recommend that you order in the morning or for the next day to make sure that your preferred delivery time is available.
Do your meals fit into a health-conscious lifestyle?

Definitely. While we’re not a diet or calorie-restrictive service, we promote cooking from fresh ingredients, providing you with the transparency and the control over what you eat. It is undoubtedly better than relying on restaurant food, take out or any ready-to-cook meals.

Our Meisterdish chefs and nutritionists curate menus so you can eat healthy, well-balanced meals with seasonal, responsibly sourced ingredients.
Cancel or Change Your Order

We know life is unpredictable. You can cancel an item or the entire order as late as 10AM on the day of your delivery sending us an email to contact@meisterdish.com. If you miss that cutoff, please call us at (917) 388-3566 and we'll try to accommodate you as best we can. Refunds can take up to 3 days before they are reflected on your payment method. Applicable delivery charges are also included in your refund.

What do I need to cook a Meisterdish meal?

You only need a basic equipped kitchen. If you have the following you are set for all of our Meisterdishes:

Salt, pepper and olive oil. We recommend a pepper mill, fresh ground pepper enhances the taste.

A stove with two flames, two pans, two pots, knife and cutting board. Wooden spoon, strainer, spatula and two mixing bowls.

If you do not have all of these at home do not worry, be creative! Most things can be improvised. You will also see in the details of every menu item what you specifically will need in your kitchen.

What are the meals like?

Our chefs select the freshest, seasonal ingredients. Often, they will source their ingredients from local, sustainable farms. You can easily know what goes into your meal by going to the meal description and clicking on the various ingredients and sources. What are the portions like?

A typical meal consists of multiple items including an entrée and one or more side dishes. Each meal comes in a minimum serving size of two adult portions.

For a restaurant-like dinner experience, we recommend to get for 2 people 2 boxes, combine one salad dish with one pasta or entrée dish. If you are looking for a quick weekday dinner, one box with two serving sizes will be fine for 2 persons. The calorie count on the boxes are per serving size.
What if I have a food allergy?

We take great care to ensure that all of our food is prepared safely. However, please note that meals are prepared and packed in a kitchen whose environment contains nuts, wheat, dairy, etc. Though best practices are used when preparing the meal, inadvertent cross-contamination may occur. As a result, we do not recommend that you order Meisterdish, if you have a sensitive food allergy.
Do you have a gluten-free plan?

A lot of our recipes don't contain gluten, however, all food is processed in a facility that may contain traces of gluten and we do not have a specifically gluten-free or Paleo diet plan.

Substitutions of ingredients from your home kitchen can help make recipes fit your dietary needs. Feel free to reach out to us at contact@meisterdish.com if you need suggestions.

DELIVERY

Do I need to be home?

No, you do not have to be at home to receive your Meisterdish, you can have your order left on your door-step or with the door man. But remember, our food is very fresh and we ensure that it stays fresh until it reaches your door step. For sustainability reasons, our packaging is minimal and does not include insulation or cooler packs as it is not needed for the typical delivery.

You will need to decide whether you are just a block away and it is fine to have the food wait for you at your door step, or ask your front desk whether they can put it in a fridge for you.

For special drop-off instructions (e.g. leaving your order at your door-step in a cooler), please let us know in the “Driver Instructions” field during checkout.

You can also place a cooler outside your home for us to drop-off your delivery. We will place your meal boxes inside the cooler. Please make sure the cooler has sufficient cooling packs to keep the meals within the temperature of food safety guidelines.

Once your order has been dropped off, you will receive a text or phone call notifying you of the delivery.
Requirements for Cooler Drop-off

You must provide a cooler with an inner compartment measuring at least 10"(w) x 12"(l) x 12"(h). It must be in good condition with a tight seal. If you order more than two Meisterdish boxes, we suggest a larger cooler. Place this cooler in a safe, secure, and shaded location.

We strongly recommend placing 1-2 ice or cooling packs in the cooler as well. A well-sealed cooler can maintain a safe temperature for over a day. It's your responsibility to ensure a safe and secure location for your cooler.
How do I handle gratuity for the delivery?

Gratuity is completely optional. If you would like to extend a tip, you can do so with either cash upon delivery, or specify an amount during the checkout process.
What do I do if my delivery does not come?

In case your delivery did not come after 45min if you ordered on-demand “NOW”, or past your scheduled delivery slot, you should call our hotline at (917) 388-3566 and we will straighten this out for you right away.
On-Demand Delivery Restrictions

Once placed, on-demand delivery orders are considered final. If you would like to order an additional menu item, you must place a second order (additional delivery charges may apply).
Will my food stay fresh in transit?

Yes, our entire process is designed for maximum freshness. Our delivery drivers are equipped with cooler boxes that ensure the optimum temperature after leaving our kitchen to your door-step. We recommend you put the Meistedish boxes in your refrigerator as soon as you receive them to maintain the freshness of your ingredients. Our meals are designed to be cooked either on the same day or next day to make sure you taste the freshness of the ingredients. We take pride in our uniquely fresh supply chain. Your ingredients will not be waiting on the grocery aisle or warm up whilst you stand in a cue and make your way home.
How long can I keep a Meisterdish meal box in the refrigerator?

We recommend to cook straight away or next day to benefit from the freshness of our ingredients. Some of the ingredients might stay fresh longer.

We have founded Meisterdish to allow you to only order when you really want to cook. No more full fridges, food waste or leftovers. Meisterdish meals are fresher, tastier and healthier than any other dinner option.

RECIPES & INGREDIENTS

What comes in each delivery?

Your Meisterdish delivery comes with all the ingredients you need, in exactly the right proportions to cook your dinner. What is special about Meisterdish is that it is designed to maximize the result but minimize the effort you need to put in. Our ingredients come cleaned, cut and diced, so that you can cook in 15 minutes (or less). To preserve maximum freshness we generally do not cut herbs and some types of fruit and vegetables.
